# Shopping Analysis Dashboard
## Introduction
The Shopping Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of customer purchasing behaviour across different demographics, seasons, payment methods, and product categories. The goal of this project is to analyse sales trends and customer preferences to support data-driven decision-making in retail management.
________________________________________
## Objectives
•	Analyse spending patterns across different age groups and product categories\
•	Identify the most preferred shipping and payment methods\
•	Evaluate the impact of seasonal discounts on sales\
•	Understand customer preferences in terms of product sizes\
•	Examine customer buying frequency patterns
________________________________________
## Key Insights
### Spending by Age and Category
•	Middle Adults contribute the highest spending overall.\
•	Clothing is the most purchased category across all age groups.\
•	Accessories and footwear show moderate demand, while outerwear has the lowest sales.
### Shipping Mode
•	Free shipping is the most preferred option.\
•	Express and store pickup options show moderate usage.\
•	Customers are price-sensitive and prefer cost-saving delivery methods.
### Payment Mode
•	Credit cards and PayPal are the most commonly used payment methods.\
•	Digital and cash payments also contribute significantly.\
•	Multiple payment options enhance customer convenience.
### Seasonal Discount Sales
•	Sales remain relatively stable across seasons.\
•	Slight increases are observed during Spring and Winter, indicating effective promotional campaigns.
### Orders by Size
•	Medium (M) size dominates orders (largest share).\
•	Followed by Large (L) and Small (S).\
•	Extra Large (XL) has the lowest demand.
### Customer Buying Frequency
•	Most customers shop every 3 months or annually.\
•	This suggests customers prefer planned or seasonal shopping rather than frequent small purchases.
________________________________________
## Conclusion
The analysis highlights that middle-aged customers purchasing clothing items with free shipping and digital payment options form the core customer segment. Seasonal promotions positively impact sales, and medium-sized products are in highest demand.
